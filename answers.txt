Question located: Compare Swing and AWT elements
AWT
Platform-dependent: Uses native OS components.
Basic components: Buttons, text fields.
Heavyweight: Less flexible and slower.

Swing
Platform-independent: Written in pure Java.
Rich components: Tables, trees, sliders.
Lightweight: More flexible, customizable.
